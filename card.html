Red Panda

Like giant pandas, red pandas have what appear to be false thumbs, but are actually just extended wrist bones.

  • Scientific Name: Ailurus fulgens
  • Habitat: Eastern Himalayas and South-western China
  • Average Lifespan: Up to 15 years
  • Average Size: 20-26 inches, 12-20 lbs
  • Diet: Omnivore, primarily bamboo

Red pandas are nocturnal mammals whom are unfortunately endangered, as there are less than 10,000 remaining in the wild. Despite being named pandas and having similar markings and colors to a raccoon, red pandas exist in a unique family all their own.
